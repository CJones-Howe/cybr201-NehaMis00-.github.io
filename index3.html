Character Profiles

Raj Malhotra

Raj is a charismatic, carefree young man from London, embodying a blend of Western freedom and deep-rooted Indian values.

Simran Singh

Simran is a traditional but imaginative young woman raised in a strict household.

Chaudhary Baldev Singh

Simran's authoritarian father who holds fast to Indian traditions.

Lajjo Singh

Simran's kind-hearted mother, torn between her husband’s authority and her daughter's happiness.

Dharamvir Malhotra

Raj’s fun-loving and progressive father who supports his son’s dreams.

Supporting Characters

Kuljeet Singh: Simran’s brash fiancé.
Preeti Singh: Kuljeet’s sister.
Chutki: Simran’s playful younger sister.

Character Arcs

Raj Malhotra

Starts as a mischievous flirt, grows into a respectful and determined man. He chooses to win over Simran’s family rather than elope, showing maturity.

Simran Singh

Begins as obedient and dutiful, but gradually gains the strength to fight for her own happiness.

Chaudhary Baldev Singh

Transforms from a rigid traditionalist to a father who honors his daughter’s love.

Lajjo Singh

Quietly empowers Simran behind the scenes, delivering the film’s iconic line of support.

Dharamvir Malhotra

Provides comic relief and wise support for Raj’s pursuit of love.
